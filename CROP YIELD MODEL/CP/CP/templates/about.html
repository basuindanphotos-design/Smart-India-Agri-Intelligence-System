About the Platform

Crop Yield Prediction & Analytics

An intelligent agricultural analytics platform that converts raw climate and farming data into production forecasts — helping farmers, researchers, and analysts make smarter, evidence-driven decisions.

Our Mission

Agriculture feeds the world, yet most smallholder farmers lack access to data-driven tools to anticipate yield outcomes before the harvest season. This platform bridges that gap by combining publicly available environmental datasets with machine learning regression models, delivering actionable yield forecasts through a clean and accessible web interface.

Machine Learning Core

Trained on multi-country agricultural records. The model learns patterns across climate, pesticide usage, and geography to output calibrated yield estimates.

Interactive Analytics

Live Chart.js visualizations powered by the real training dataset — explore crop distributions, rainfall correlations, temperature impacts, and historical trends.
