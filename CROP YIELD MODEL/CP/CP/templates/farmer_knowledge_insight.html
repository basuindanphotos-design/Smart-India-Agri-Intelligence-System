Seasonal Action Guide

  • Land Preparation: Test soil pH and organic carbon before sowing.
  • Sowing Window: Match sowing date with expected rainfall onset in your area.
  • Irrigation: Use split irrigation during critical growth stages (flowering and grain fill).
  • Nutrient Strategy: Apply basal NPK and top-dress nitrogen in 2-3 splits.
  • Pest Management: Prefer threshold-based spray decisions over fixed schedules.

Climate Risk Alerts

Heat Stress Risk

If daytime temperature exceeds 35 C during flowering, expect reduced yield potential.

Rainfall Deficit

If rainfall is below seasonal normal, prioritize irrigation in reproductive stages.

Soil Moisture Conservation

Mulching and residue retention can reduce evapotranspiration and protect yield.
