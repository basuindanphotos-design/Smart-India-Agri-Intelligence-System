About the Project

AI-powered crop recommendation system for farmer development and explainable decisions.

What this platform does

It recommends the best crop from your input profile and environmental factors, then explains why that recommendation is suitable.

Why this is industry-level

  • Separate pages for dataset, preprocessing, EDA, model evaluation and prediction flow.
  • CSV upload, append/replace, export and paginated dataset exploration.
  • Model metrics and transparency reports for trust and governance.
  • Farmer-focused education panel covering irrigation, soil, fertilizer and weather impact.

Tech Stack

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Chart.js
  • Backend: Flask + Pandas + scikit-learn
  • Models: Crop classifier + yield regressor pipelines
