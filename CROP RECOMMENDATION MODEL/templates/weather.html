About Weather Data

How weather affects your crop selection and recommendations.

Temperature Impact

Temperature directly affects crop growth rate and productivity. Different crops have optimal temperature ranges. Too high or too low temperatures can stress plants and reduce yield significantly.

Humidity Levels

Humidity affects plant transpiration and disease pressure. High humidity increases fungal disease risk, while low humidity increases water requirement and stress on crops. Balance is critical for health.

Rainfall Patterns

Rainfall is crucial for rainfed agriculture. Timing and quantity determine irrigation needs. Excessive rainfall can cause waterlogging, while insufficient rainfall requires supplementary irrigation.

Weather Variability

Unexpected weather changes can impact crop performance. Our system accounts for seasonal patterns and helps you prepare for optimal conditions and manage risks effectively.

Crop Selection

Matching crop type with local weather conditions is essential for success. Our AI learns from weather patterns to recommend crops that will thrive in your specific environment.

Decision Making

Real-time weather data enables data-driven farming decisions. Plan irrigation, apply fertilizers, and schedule operations based on actual weather conditions for better results.
